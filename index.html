Fitbit Charge 2

January 31, 2017 by Adena Bauer

WHAT


The Fitbit Charge 2 is amongst the latest products released from Fitbit. The sleek wrist-wearable tracks your heart rate 24/7, calculates calories burned, automatically detects exercises, tracks and evaluates your sleep, notifies you for calls, texts and alerts, and offers guided breathing sessions.


The accompanying Fitbit App helps you set your goals and track your nutrition, hydration and fitness levels.

WHY


While I admit that am that I am one of the many New Year’s resolution-ers who flocked to buy a Fitbit and commit to a healthier 2017, I am confident that I'll be wearing the device throughout the new years to come.


Fitbit Charge 2 takes an honest and holistic approach to your health.

HONEST


Fitbit’s honest approach is a refreshing break from what we normally see in the Health and Fitness Industry. Many fitness products take the standpoint that you either are or are not fit. The products communicate that you don’t look good yet. These products are a nagging reminder of imperfections and the user becomes focused on proving the product wrong. This kind of "guilt driven" business model creates a dishonest product.


Fitbit is honest. The Charge 2 app focuses on where you stand against your own goals. The device is personal and keeps you accountable with an organized and discrete display of where you have been successful and where you have been off track. Fitbit doesn’t make bold, inauthentic claims about looking better. It uses real data — calories burned, steps walked, resting heart rate — and friendly reminders to empower you with the tools to achieve your fitness goals.


The Fitbit Charge 2 is honest because users make their own decisions about their fitness, leaving them powerful and already successful.

HOLISTIC


The mind and spirit are as important as the body in the journey towards sustainable health. The Charge 2 takes a holistic approach to fitness because it includes sleep tracking for a sharper mind and guided deep breathing sessions for a happier spirit.


Fitbit’s attention to the user is vital to the success of Charge 2 as a health and fitness product. The sleeping and breathing features remind users that balance is key. Fitbit’s holistic approach stands out amongst its competitors, who often focus on activity and nutrition only, failing to keep the user’s most desired outcome in mind: fulfillment.


Additionally, Fitbit Charge 2 recognizes that its users have vibrant lives that aren’t defined by their fitness goals. For this reason, the Fitbit Charge 2 wearable is fun and sleek. It has interchangeable bands so users can sport different colors and styles and bring self expression to their health.


The Fitbit Charge 2 is holistic in its approach, empowering users to stay focused on the mind, body and spirit.

HYPOTHESIS


The Fitbit Charge 2 tracks how users are meeting their fitness and health goals, but it fails to give an explanation of why.


Fitbit constantly collects data about sleep, nutrition, hydration, relaxation, and exercise. There are insights to gather about why an individual achieves her goals. It’s very possible, for example, that a good night's sleep is one's highest indicator of exercising. For others, drinking plenty of water is the highest indicator. Everyone has different patterns.


If an individual knew why she achieved her goals, she would better accomplish them. I conducted a survey to test my hypothesis. I asked USC students if they were interested in learning what contributes to a good night’s sleep, motivation to exercise, better eating, and an overall better mood.

SURVEY RESULTS


Survey of 32 active students (excercising at least twice a week) between the ages of 18 and 25.


RECOMMENDATION


I found that active students are interested in learning what they can do to better sleep, eat, exercise and to improve their mood.


The Fitbit Charge 2 app should use its vast amount of bodily and nutritional data, gathered from the device, to make predictive insights on how the user best accomplishes her health and fitness goals. These insights will be personal and user specific, heightening the product's integrity.
